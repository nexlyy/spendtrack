Privacy Policy

SpendTrack — offline personal expense tracker

Last updated: July 5, 2026

In short: SpendTrack works entirely on your device and offline. It has no internet access, collects no personal data, contains no ads, analytics or trackers, and never sends your information anywhere. Everything you enter stays on your phone.

This Privacy Policy explains how the SpendTrack mobile application (the “App”, “we”, “our”) handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy. We wrote it to be short and honest, because there is genuinely very little to disclose: the App is designed so your financial data never leaves your device.

1Who we are

SpendTrack is an independent, privacy-first budgeting tool that helps you record expenses, income, budgets and savings goals, and optionally import a bank statement file to sort transactions into categories. There is no account, no sign-up, and no cloud service behind the App.

2What data we collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent or share any personal data. The App requests no INTERNET permission, so it is technically unable to send your data anywhere. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no crash-reporting services and no third-party trackers embedded in the App.

Because we collect nothing, there is no personal profile of you, no usage history on our side, and nothing for us to lose in a data breach. We simply never receive your information in the first place.

3Where your data is stored

All information you create in the App — expense and income records, notes, categories, budgets, savings goals, recurring payments, exchange rates and app settings — is stored locally on your device only, inside the App’s private on-device database (IndexedDB). This data:

  • never leaves your device automatically;
  • is not accessible to us or to any third party;
  • is removed when you delete the App or use the in-app “Delete all data” option.

4Backups you create yourself

The App includes a “Save backup” feature. When you use it, the App generates a file containing your data and hands it to your device’s standard save/share dialog. You choose what happens to that file — for example saving it to local storage, or sending it to your own cloud drive. The App does not upload this file anywhere on its own and has no server to upload it to. Any storage or cloud provider you personally choose is governed by that provider’s own privacy policy, not ours.

To keep your data private, the App also disables Android’s automatic cloud backup, so your database is not copied off the device without your explicit action.

5Importing bank statements

You may optionally import a bank statement (CSV or XLSX) that you exported yourself. The file is read and parsed entirely on your device to recognise merchants and assign categories. Statement contents are never uploaded, and the file is not retained by the App beyond the import — only the resulting expense/income records you choose to keep are saved locally.

6Permissions

The App does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos or any other sensitive permission. When you pick a file to import or restore, this happens through your operating system’s file picker, which grants the App one-time access to the single file you selected and no persistent access to your storage.

7Third parties and advertising

There are no third-party services, no advertising networks, no in-app purchases and no external content loaded from the internet. The App is fully self-contained.

8Children’s privacy

The App is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country).

9Data security

Because your data stays on your device and is never transmitted, its security is tied to the security of your own device. We recommend protecting your phone with a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date. Note that a backup file you export is a plain data file — store it somewhere you trust.

10Your rights

You are always in full control of your data. You can view, edit, export or permanently delete all of it at any time from within the App, without asking us, because it lives only on your device. Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct or erase on our side.

11International users

The App processes data only locally on your device, in the country where you use it. No cross-border transfer of your personal data to us or to any server takes place, because no such transfer happens at all.

12Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect new features. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
